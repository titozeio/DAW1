## Mechas
## Estilo visual:  
Una mezcla entre Battletech (realistas, occidentales, orientados a la guerra, y con sensación de desgaste, de tecnología estancada) y Gundam (un poco más humanoides y japonizados). 

### Saber Prime
Refleja su diseño de otra época, con aspecto más humanoide al estar pensado originalmente para ser pilotado por un humano, y su equipamiento principal.

- **Vertical Image:**
    - **Prompt:** A vertical portrait concept art of an older generation humanoid combat mecha. Mixing Battletech realism, wear-and-tear, and stagnant technology aesthetics with prominent Gundam proportions. Designed originally for human pilots but retrofitted for AI. It is wielding a intensely glowing plasma saber and a small plasma cannon. Military weathered colors. Ruined battlefield background at sunset. No text, no letters, no logos.

### Bullseye
Mantiene las modificaciones que hablamos (camuflaje, ojo de Shockwave) junto con su rol de "Glass Cannon" con cañones automáticos en lugar de brazos.

- **Vertical Image:**
    - **Prompt:** A vertical portrait concept art of a heavy sniper mecha. Mixing Battletech realism, wear-and-tear, and stagnant technology aesthetics with subtle Gundam proportions. Woodland camouflage colors with dark brown stripes. The head has a single glowing red eye similar to Transformers Shockwave. It has dual heavy autocannons instead of arms. Prominent thrusters on the back. Ruined battlefield background at sunset. No text, no letters, no logos.
    - **Picture:** ![Bullseye](art/concepts/BullseyeBig1.png)

### Bullwark
Conocido como "DOC", incorpora los elementos industriales que comentamos para reflejar su capacidad de reparar aliados, junto a su cañón láser en el hombro derecho.

- **Vertical Image:**
    - **Prompt:** A vertical portrait concept art of a heavy repair mecha. Mixing Battletech realism, wear-and-tear, and stagnant technology aesthetics. Painted in industrial yellow and orange colors with hazard stripes. It has a physical crane and repair tools integrated into its chassis. A small laser cannon is mounted on its right shoulder. Ruined battlefield background at sunset. No text, no letters, no logos.
    - **Picture:** ![Bulwark](art/concepts/BullwarkBig1.png)

### Scout
Diseñado como un robot pequeño y ágil para explorar, con retropropulsores en la espalda y el brazo derecho sustituido por un cañón automático.

- **Vertical Image:**
    - **Prompt:** A vertical portrait concept art of a light and agile reconnaissance mecha. Mixing Battletech realism, wear-and-tear, and stagnant technology aesthetics. Slender chassis with light armor. The entire right arm is replaced by a massive heavy autocannon. Prominent mobility retro-thrusters visible on the back. Military weathered colors. Ruined battlefield background at sunset. No text, no letters, no logos.

### Death Knight
Diseño de mole pesada con camuflaje negro nocturno, portando un martillo gigante a dos manos propulsado por cohetes, lo que le impide llevar otras armas.

- **Vertical Image:**
    - **Prompt:** A vertical portrait concept art of a massive, heavily armored juggernaut mecha. Mixing Battletech realism, wear-and-tear, and stagnant technology aesthetics. Painted in dark black night-camouflage. Wielding a gigantic two-handed mechanical melee hammer with integrated rocket thrusters on the hammer head. It carries no firearms. Ruined battlefield background at night. No text, no letters, no logos.

### Ice Age
Diseño angular y agresivo con colores azul y blanco sin camuflaje, portando un rifle congelador a dos manos y afustes de misiles en los hombros.

- **Vertical Image:**
    - **Prompt:** A vertical portrait concept art of an angular and aggressive combat mecha. Mixing Battletech realism, wear-and-tear, and stagnant technology aesthetics. Uncamouflaged stark blue and white color scheme. Holding a large, heavy sci-fi freeze-ray rifle with both hands. Two large cluster missile pods mounted on the shoulders. Frozen ruined battlefield background. No text, no letters, no logos.
